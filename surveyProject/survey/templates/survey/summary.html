Even after just few analysis we can see how dynamic the industry is:

  1. Age question in 2020
    Every 4th respondent did not want to answer how old he/she was
  2. Age question in general
    One third of the respondents declared age between 25 and 34
  3. Level of education question
    More than 40% of the respondents got a bachelor`s degree, only 20% - master`s degree
  4. Language to have worked with in 2020 question
    More than a half of the respondents declared to have worked with JavaScript and HTML/CSS.
  5. Language want to work with next year in 2020 question
    42% of the respondents planned to learn Python and JavaScript.
  6. Language to have worked with in 2023 question
    More than 62% of the respondents declared to have worked with JavaScript and 52% with HTML/CSS
  7. Language want to work with next year in 2023 question
    Approximately 39% of the respondents planned to learn Python and JavaScript.
  8. Current job question in 2020
    More than 30% of the respondents were back-end Developer, 10% were full-stack Developer, 5% as Database administrator
  9. Current job question in 2023
    More than 30% of the respondents were full-stack Developer, 18% were back-end Developer, 6% as front-end Developer. Only less than 1% declared Database administrator job
  10. Current job question in general
    In 2023 new jobs appears comparing to 2020, like: Security professional, Cloud infrastructure engineer, Research & Development role
